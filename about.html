Introduction to the Project

P.R.I.M.O. stands for Precious Responsible Intelligent Manav Observant.

It is a smart robotic creation made to behave and assist like a human companion.

The goal of this project is to blend intelligence with responsibility, creating a machine that helps society while encouraging sustainability and creativity.
