Uses of P.R.I.M.O. in Different Places

  • School – Helps teachers and interacts with students.
  • Home – Personal assistant for reminders and monitoring.
  • Hospital – Assists doctors with patient observation.
  • Office – Automates announcements and tasks.
  • Old Age Home – Provides companionship and reminders.
  • Shopping Mall – Guides and informs visitors.
  • Research Lab – Helps collect data and monitor experiments.
  • Railway/Airport – Assists in directions and security alerts.
  • Defense Area – Works as a patrol and surveillance robot.
  • Public Place – Gives directions and safety instructions.
